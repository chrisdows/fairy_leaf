Fairy Friends of the Leaf

"In homes with leaves upon their gate,
A fairy charm, a happy fate.
Where love and joy are sure to be,
And laughter rings as wild as free.

The fairies know, the fairies see,
That leaf is sign of harmony.
So now, dear friend, please understand,
Your house is magic, pure and grand.

For fairies visit all year long,
To fill your home with dance and song.
And every time they spy that leaf,
They leave behind a bit of peace."
